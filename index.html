The Spells

When you level up, you can create your own spell! Types of spells:

  • Name: Give your Spell a Name
  • Type: Damaging (deals damage to your opponent), Healing (restores Health), Enhancing (permanently increases an attribute)
  • Power: Scale the bar to set the cost and power of the Spell
  • Upgrade: Choose one Attribute to upgrade. How every Attribute influences your Character is shown in the stats menu ('s')

You get a +1 on the Attribute you choose on every Spell you create. If you choose Enhancing as a Type, you won't get a new skill but the increase in stats will be much higher. If you chose a physical Attribute (Strength, Dexterity) your Mana will permanently decline!


Enemies

You get Experience by fighting enemies (Blue = regular; Green = Boss). If Enemies walk into each other, they combine and become much stronger, so take care of them quick! To clear the Level you need to defeat the Boss (green). The grey squares are Mana Potions. After defeating every Enemy, beside the Boss, in a dungeon, you'll get new equipment.
